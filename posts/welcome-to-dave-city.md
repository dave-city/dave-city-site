# Welcome to Dave City

Welcome to **Dave City** — the new community site for Dave Madson Entertainment Inc.!

Since 2011, Dave Madson has been providing the community with weird and funny bloopers of classic television and film logos. From Looney Tunes and Merrie Melodies to Viacom's V of Doom, the Screen Gems S from Hell, PBS, CBS, and NET — no classic logo is safe.

This site is a hub for the Dave Madson fan community. Here you'll find:

- The latest videos from the [Dave Madson Entertainment Inc. YouTube channel](https://www.youtube.com/channel/UCGBiNqgNTsNUgRkUvAr4jWA)
- Community resources and links
- Open source projects from the [Dave City GitHub organization](https://github.com/dave-city)

Thanks for visiting, and stay tuned for more logo blooper madness!
