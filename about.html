About Dave City

Dave Madson has been the logo blooper pioneer since 2011, bringing the community weird and funny bloopers of classic television and film logos.

Who Is Dave Madson?

Since 2011, Dave Madson has been providing the community with weird and funny bloopers of classic television and film logos. These videos, available via the Dave Madson Entertainment Inc. YouTube channel, include various text-to-speech-voiced characters that have become beloved by fans around the world.

From Looney Tunes and Merrie Melodies to Viacom’s V of Doom, the Screen Gems S from Hell, PBS, CBS, and NET — no classic logo is safe from the blooper treatment.

The Characters

Microsoft Sam & Friends

The core cast of text-to-speech voices including Microsoft Sam, Mike, Anna, and Mary — the voices behind the chaos.

Radar Overseer Scotty

Along with Randa, Abbie, and Beulah, these characters keep things interesting with their unique personalities.

Lernout & Hauspie Voices

Michael and Michelle from the Lernout & Hauspie text-to-speech engine bring their own brand of comedy to the bloopers.

Heroes & Villains

Mr. Warner, Harvey Zilf the news reporter, the Devil, Shedevil, and plenty of other evil beings — plus God and his angels to beat back the evildoers.

What Is Dave City?

Dave City is the community site for Dave Madson fans. Here you can find the latest videos, community resources, and open source projects. This site is maintained by the community on GitHub.
